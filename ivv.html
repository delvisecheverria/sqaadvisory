What is IV&V?

Independent oversight for critical software decisions.

Independent Verification & Validation gives leaders an objective view of software quality, delivery risk, and release confidence throughout the software lifecycle.

Verification: built correctly

We evaluate whether the solution is being built according to requirements, standards, specifications, governance expectations, and quality controls.

Validation: built for the business

We assess whether the solution meets business needs, supports user outcomes, reduces operational risk, and is ready for successful deployment.
